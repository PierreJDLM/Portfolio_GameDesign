Echoes of Eternity

Echoes of Eternity is a mind-bending narrative puzzle game that challenges players to navigate through time-warped landscapes while unraveling an intricate story. As the lead designer, I was responsible for:

  • Crafting complex, multi-layered puzzles that integrate seamlessly with the time manipulation mechanics
  • Designing levels that tell a story through environmental storytelling and subtle clues
  • Balancing difficulty progression to ensure a challenging yet rewarding experience
  • Collaborating with the narrative team to weave the story into every aspect of the gameplay

The game received critical acclaim for its innovative use of time mechanics and its thought-provoking narrative, earning a 90% rating on GameCritic.

Neon Nexus

Neon Nexus is an electrifying multiplayer racing game set in a neon-drenched cyberpunk world. My contributions to this project included:

  • Designing an addictive progression system that keeps players engaged for hours
  • Creating a deep vehicle customization system that allows for millions of unique combinations
  • Balancing the gameplay to ensure fair and exciting races for players of all skill levels
  • Implementing social features to foster a strong community around the game

Neon Nexus has become a popular esports title, with over 5 million active monthly players and regular tournaments featuring prize pools of up to $1 million.

Whispers of the Wild

Whispers of the Wild is an enlightening adventure game that immerses players in the world of wildlife conservation. My role in this project involved:

  • Developing core gameplay mechanics that simulate real-world conservation efforts
  • Designing intricate animal behavior systems for a truly authentic experience
  • Creating educational content that's seamlessly integrated into the gameplay
  • Collaborating with wildlife experts to ensure accuracy and respect for nature

The game has been praised for its educational value and has been adopted by several schools as part of their environmental science curriculum. It also won the "Games for Change" award in 2022.
